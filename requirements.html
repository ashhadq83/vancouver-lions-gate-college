Admission Requirements

Vancouver Lions Gate College

Vancouver Lions Gate College is committed to providing high-quality vocational education in a supportive and innovative environment. We focus on delivering industry-relevant programs that equip students with the skills they need to thrive in their careers. With a commitment to academic excellence and personal development, we aim to help our students achieve both their educational and professional goals.

Our programs are designed with real-world applications in mind, ensuring that our graduates are job-ready upon completion. We offer a variety of career-focused programs, each tailored to meet the needs of our diverse student body and the ever-changing demands of the job market.

Admission Requirements

  • Applicants must be older than 16 years old.
  • Applicants must provide the school with a copy of their birth certificate or an identification document from the government, such as a passport, citizen card, or permanent resident card to be accepted into the course of study.
  • Applicants must read and certify that they have read a copy of the school’s policies and procedures, any updates and inserts, and the enrolment and registration agreement.
  • Students under the age of 19 years must have their contract counter-signed by their parent or legal guardian.
  • All Applicants must meet the above criteria, pay their Tuition deposits in full, and sign their Official Enrollment Agreements before they are considered officially enrolled in a program.
