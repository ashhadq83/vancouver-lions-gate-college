Nail Technician Diploma Program

Program Details

The Nail Technician Diploma Program is a comprehensive 300-hour course that offers both theoretical instruction and hands-on practical training. Students will gain skills in a variety of key areas such as Manicuring and Pedicures, Artificial Nails, Nail Art, Professional Practices, Sanitation and Sterilisation, Safety, Salon Management, Anatomy and Physiology.

This program is designed to provide students with a deep understanding of the nail care industry, preparing them for success in a professional salon environment. With a strong emphasis on practical application, students will have ample opportunities to practice and perfect their skills. Students with exceptional performance may even be given the opportunity to stay on with our store for real-world experience.

Tuition Fees

The tuition fees for the Nail Technician Diploma Program are as follows:

  • Domestic Students: $3800 for tuition + $250 for textbooks + $450 for course materials
  • International Students: $6500 for tuition + $250 for textbooks + $450 for course materials

The total program cost includes theory lessons, practical training, textbooks, and course materials. This is a PTIB (Private Training Institutions Branch) approved program, ensuring a high standard of education and certification.

Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive the Nail Technician Diploma. This certification will prepare you to enter the nail care industry as a professional nail technician with the skills and knowledge required to succeed in the field.

Our experienced instructor, with over 10 years in the nail industry, ensures that students receive top-quality education and training that meets industry standards.
